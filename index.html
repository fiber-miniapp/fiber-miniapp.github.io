Overview

Fiber is a suite of miniapps that are maintained and developed at RIKEN Advanced Institute for Computational Science (RIKEN AICS).

Miniapps

  • CCS QCD: A QCD miniapp originally developed by Kenichi Ishikawa (Hiroshima University), et al. [Download source][View repository]
